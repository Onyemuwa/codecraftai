Questions? We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know about Codecraft AI

Is Codecraft AI truly autonomous?

Yes. Our AI agents simulate real hacker behavior without human intervention. However, all tests are monitored by our security team for safety and quality assurance. The AI learns from each scan, improving its attack simulations over time.

Is it safe and non-destructive?

Absolutely. We only perform external, read-only testing. We never modify data, execute destructive payloads, or attempt denial-of-service attacks. All scans are designed to identify vulnerabilities without causing harm to your systems.

What vulnerabilities do you cover?

OWASP Top 10, business logic flaws, configuration errors, and advanced web vulnerabilities. This includes SQL injection, XSS, CSRF, insecure deserialization, SSRF, and more. See our full coverage list in documentation.

How do you handle our data?

We never store sensitive data. Test targets are encrypted in transit and at rest. We're GDPR compliant and can sign NDAs for enterprise clients. All data is deleted after 30 days unless you choose to retain it.

Can this replace traditional pentests?

For most startups and growing companies, yes. For compliance requiring manual testing (like certain PCI DSS requirements), we complement traditional pentests with continuous coverage. Many teams use us for regular testing and bring in manual testers annually for compliance.

What if I find a false positive?

Flag it directly in the report with one click. Our AI learns from feedback, improving accuracy over time. You can also adjust scan sensitivity in settings to reduce false positives for your specific environment.

How do I fix the vulnerabilities you find?

Every finding includes step-by-step remediation guides, code snippets, and references to documentation. For critical issues, we provide direct code examples in multiple languages (Python, JavaScript, Java, etc.) and can even generate security patches for common frameworks.

Can I test staging/development environments?

Yes, unlimited staging and test environment scans are included in all paid plans. We recommend testing staging before production deployments to catch vulnerabilities early in your development cycle.
